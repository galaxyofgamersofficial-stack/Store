Refund Policy

Our Refund Policy

At MineFire Store, we strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction with our digital Minecraft products. Due to the digital nature of our products, we have specific refund policies in place.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • If the product was not delivered due to a technical error on our end
  • If the product received is significantly different from what was described
  • If the product is completely non-functional and our support team cannot resolve the issue

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • If you change your mind after purchase
  • If you didn't read the product description properly before purchasing
  • If the product doesn't work as expected due to your system configuration
  • If you've already used the product for an extended period
  • If the issue can be resolved through our customer support

Refund Timeframe

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited to your original payment method.

Important Notes

Please Note

All refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We reserve the right to deny refunds that don't meet our policy criteria. In cases where a refund is granted, access to the product will be revoked.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with:

  • Your order ID
  • The email address used for purchase
  • A detailed explanation of why you're requesting a refund
  • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, etc.)
